About me

Public health data, surveillance, and immunization analytics.

My professional focus is transforming complex health data into evidence for timely decisions, stronger surveillance, and better immunization programme performance.

I work in the World Health Organization Eastern Mediterranean Region as a Data Management Specialist supporting the Immunization, Vaccines and Polio programme. My work includes surveillance data quality assessment, subnational coverage analysis, outbreak threshold calculations, GIS mapping, dashboard development, and reporting for measles, rubella, rotavirus, invasive bacterial diseases, and maternal and neonatal tetanus.

I bring a multidisciplinary background that combines pharmacy, field epidemiology, biostatistics, healthcare quality, data management, and international public health. I am especially interested in using Generative AI responsibly to improve data cleaning, analysis, visualization, reporting, and knowledge synthesis workflows.

About the course

An 8-week guided bootcamp in practical, responsible AI.

The course focuses on no-code AI tools for responsible task automation, research productivity, scientific communication, data analysis, and final project development.

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity

The bootcamp combines weekly live workshops, help sessions, community forums, personalized feedback, capstone coaching, a shareable certificate, and lifetime access to a tutorial library.
